Power BI dashboard for NBAKaggle2024 data set:
- Total players
- Total TRADED players
- Count players by position
- Average age by team
- Average PTS by player
- Average Minutes played by player
- Average 2p% by playey
- Filtered by players who are NOT 2TM(Players marked as 2TM played for two teams during the season. The 2TM row already represents the combined season totals for that player, while their individual team-level stats also exist in the dataset)
